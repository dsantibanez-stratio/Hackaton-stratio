# Stratio Brand — Word Document (.docx) Guidelines

Always read `/mnt/skills/public/docx/SKILL.md` for technical implementation details.
Apply the following Stratio-specific settings on top of that skill.

> **Regla de logo:** fondo blanco → `assets/logo-dark.png` · cualquier otro fondo → `assets/logo-white.png`

## Page Setup
- Page size: A4
- Margins: Top 2.5cm, Bottom 2.5cm, Left 3cm, Right 2.5cm
- Background: White (`#FFFFFF`)

## Styles

### Title (document cover or first heading)
- Font: Inter Bold, 28pt
- Color: Space 17 (`#0F1B27`)
- Spacing after: 12pt
- Add a 3pt bottom border in Action 6 (`#0776DF`) under the title

### Heading 1
- Font: Inter SemiBold, 18pt
- Color: Space 17 (`#0F1B27`)
- Spacing before: 18pt, after: 6pt

### Heading 2
- Font: Inter Medium, 14pt
- Color: Action 6 (`#0776DF`)
- Spacing before: 12pt, after: 4pt

### Heading 3
- Font: Inter Medium, 12pt
- Color: Space 17 (`#0F1B27`)
- Spacing before: 8pt, after: 2pt

### Body Text
- Font: Inter Regular, 11pt
- Color: `#1A1A1A` (near-black for readability)
- Line spacing: 1.15
- Spacing after: 6pt

### Caption
- Font: Inter Light, 9pt
- Color: `#666666`

## Header
- Left: Document title or section name — Inter Light, 9pt, color `#666666`
- Right: `assets/logo-dark.png` (white background) — scale to ~2cm wide
- Separator: 0.5pt rule in Action 6 (`#0776DF`)

## Footer
- Left: "Stratio Generative AI Data Fabric" — Inter Light, 9pt, color Space 17 (`#0F1B27`)
- Right: Page number — Inter Light, 9pt, color Space 17
- Separator: 0.5pt rule in `#CCCCCC`

## Tables
- Header row: Background Action 6 (`#0776DF`), text White, Inter SemiBold 10pt
- Odd rows: White background
- Even rows: `#EBF4FD` (very light blue)
- Border: 0.5pt solid `#CCCCCC`
- Cell padding: 4pt all sides

## Cover Page (when needed)
- Full-page background: Space 17 (`#0F1B27`)
- Logo: `assets/logo-white.png` top-left, ~5cm wide  ← dark background → white logo
- Title: Inter Bold, 32pt, White
- Subtitle: Inter Light, 16pt, Action 6 (`#0776DF`)
- Date/author: Inter Light, 11pt, White at 70% opacity (`#B3CADE` approximation)
- Bottom: thin Action 6 rule, then "Stratio Generative AI Data Fabric" in small white text
